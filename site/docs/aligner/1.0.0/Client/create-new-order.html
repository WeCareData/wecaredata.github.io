We'll prepare a treatment setup representing out best attempt to achieve your prescribed goals. If you've requested that we limit the duration of treatment, this may restrict or prevent some of your other prescribed goals. In case of conflict, we will prioritize restrictions on treatment duration.

When you receive the treatment setup, you'll see the number of planned steps and the cost of planned steps and the cost of both pricing options. If you choose Flex, you will revieve only the aligners represented in the setup any revisions, replacements, or retainers will be sold separately. If you choose Ulimited, the cost of aligners and retainers will be covered for five full years (Click here for details).

You pay nothing now. You are under no obligation to continue the case, but if you do, the appropriate lab fee will be due when you approve the treatment setup.
